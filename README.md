## 1. Coca-Cola USA Retailer Dashboard

Analyzes Coca-Cola's U.S. sales performance from 2022 to 2023 across brands and major retailers.

**Key Features:**
- Year-over-year comparison by beverage brand and retailer  
- Total sales, units sold, average pricing, and profit margin  
- Quarterly sales trend with operating profit margin  
- Slicers for filtering by region and year  

**Tools Used:** Pivot Tables, Slicers, Conditional Formatting, Line & Bar Charts

- Visual insights via stacked bars and trendlines
